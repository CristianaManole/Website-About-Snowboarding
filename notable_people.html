Jake Burton Carpenter
69 years old
The co-founder of Burton Snowboards, Jake Burton Carpenter is considered one of the pioneers of modern snowboarding. His company had a significant impact on the development of snowboarding equipment and the popularization of the sport.
Shaun White
37 years old
Known as "The Flying Tomato," Shaun White is one of the most successful snowboarders of all time. He has won numerous gold medals at the X Games and has been a three-time Olympic champion in the halfpipe event.
Terje Haakonsen
48 years old
Terje Haakonsen is recognized for his major influence on extreme snowboarding and freeriding. He was a pioneer in the development of snowboard equipment and freestyle and backcountry styles.
Hannah Teter
36 years old
Hannah Teter is an American snowboarder who has won medals at the Winter Olympics and X Games. She is known for her contributions to halfpipe snowboarding and her involvement in charitable causes.
Craig Kelly
He passed away in 2003.
Craig Kelly, was one of the early top snowboarders and a significant promoter of the sport. He contributed to the development of backcountry techniques and influenced the evolution of snowboarding equipment.
